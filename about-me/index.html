Who am i?

I am from San Francisco, Zulia state, Venezuela, I was born on June 9, 2000. I have been a member of the Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints since I was born, I have been raised in the path of covenants, I have had the great blessing to have loving parents and a united family, they have both been an example to me. Since I was little I have been very interested in computers and new technologies, thanks to that I always tried to learn everything I could, I remember that I felt like a genius when I connected my old PC to the television and sound system, and installed Windows 10, among other things. Growing up I had other responsibilities and priorities, until I went to the mission in March 2019, which was Mission Venezuela-Caracas, one of the best years of my life, preaching the gospel totally helped me to know my Savior and know that he lives I returned home on March 21, 2021, and in May 2022 I married a wonderful woman, whom I am now lucky enough to call my wife, we were sealed in the temple a month later and now we are preparing to graduate and form our Family, we don't have children yet.

My main hobbies are learning as much as I can from courses and videos, playing SNES and GBA games, and spending time with my wife. Of course when my calling and work allow me. Since I currently work as a web developer and am a ward secretary, I am also a family history leader.

I am a 22-year-old young man preparing to develop his full potential.
